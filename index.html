Welcome to the First Vice President's Office

The First Vice President's Office (FVPO) is a new established office under the setup of the Government of National Unity (GNU).

This office has been created to serve the 10th ammendment of Zanzibar constitution of 1984. The mandate of FVPO is to coordinate issues pertaining to sustainable environmental management, HIV and AIDS, Disability Affairs, Substance Abuse and Rehabilitation. In regards to its role and responsibilities, the FVPO addresses issues of cross cutting nature.

Mhe: Seif Sharif Hamad

The Revolutionary Government of Zanzibar is committed to improve economic growth, enhance environmental protection and provide social protection and welfare of its people. In doing so, the government has developed Vision 2020 and The Zanzibar National Strategy for Growth and Reduction of Poverty 1 & 2 (MKUZA) which instigate economic growth and poverty alleviation. The aforementioned national development policies recognize the need for Zanzibar to have a prosperous and fair society.
