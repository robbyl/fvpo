First Vice President's Office

The First Vice President's Office (FVPO) is a new established office under the setup of the Government of National Unity (GNU).This office has been created to serve the 10th ammendment of Zanzibar constitution of 1984. The mandate of FVPO is to coordinate issues pertaining to sustainable environmental management, HIV and AIDS, Disability Affairs, Substance Abuse and Rehabilitation. In regards to its role and responsibilities, the FVPO addresses issues of cross cutting nature.FVPO is headed by the First Vice President, Minister of state the FVPO, Principal Secretary, and Deputy Principal Secretary, Directors and Commissioners. There are five departments namely, Department of Environment, Disability Affairs, Administration and Personel, Planning, Policy and Research and FVPO Private Office and Pemba's Head Office. The two commissions are Zanzibar AIDS Commission (ZAC) and The National Commission for Coordination and Drugs Control.

Vision

To have a Zanzibar society living in a sustainable environment, free from HIV and AIDS, elicit drugs and enjoys equal rights and opportunities for economic growth and wellbeing.

Mission

FVPO is envisaged to provide a coordination leadership through promotion, advocacy, creating enabling policy and legal environment towards issues related to HIV and AIDS, environment, elicit drugs and people with disabilities.

Objectives

To contribute the realization of Vision 2020 and MKUZA, the following objectives outlined here under are imperatives:

i. Sound ministerial management and accountabilities attained and sustained.

ii. Sustainable use of natural resources and environmental management promoted.

iii. Supply and availability of illicit drugs for use and trafficking eliminated.

iv. HIV and AIDS infections rate reduced and services improved.

v. The rights and equal opportunities for the Persons with Disabilities (PWDs) advocated.
