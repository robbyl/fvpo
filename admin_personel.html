Department of Administration and Personnel

General objectives

  • To supervise welfare of Employee

  • To administered employment procedures

  • To conduct training

  • To supervise Administration of the Office

  • To manage all Procurement and to store Goods

Institutional structure of the department

The Department of Planning, Policy and Research consists of six specialized sections, with the head of each section reporting to the Director.

These sections are

  • The legal officer unit

  • Human resources unit

  • Recording management unit

  • Procurement unit.

  • Office supervisor

  • Account section

(i) The legal officer unit

Function of The legal officer unit

  • Over all in charge of legal section at F VPO.

  • To coordinate over all enforcement and compliance of FVPO.

  • To coordinate all legal activities FVPO

  • To supervise coordinate legitimation concerning F VPO.

  • To prepare legal document.

  • To prepare ACTS and regulation

  • To facilitate the legal issue concerning .

(ii) Human resources unit

Function of The Human resources unit

  • Prepare jobs design..

  • To Conduct recruitment procedures.

  • To Preparation puncheon and gratuities procedure for staff who want turn off.

  • To Promote employees relation.

  • To make transfer for and for employees and allocation.

(iii) Recording management unit:

Function of Recording management unit:

  • To receive incoming mail records and keep record.

  • To sort-out incoming mail.

  • To put in the filling in the files.

  • To record outgoing mail in dispatch.

  • To prepare the index in the fillies.

  • To arrange the fillies accordingly.

(iv) Procurement unit.

Functions of Procurement unit:

  • Manage all procurement and disposal activities of the procuring and disposing entry except adjudication and the award of contracts.

  • To Recommend procurement and disposal procedure.

  • Check and prepare statement of requirement.

  • To Prepare advertisements for bid opportunities.

  • Prepare bid document.

  • Issue bidding documents

  • Maintain Provider list

  • Prepare mouthy reports for the tender board.

  • Prepare contracts.

(v) Office supervisor:

Function of Office supervisor:

  • To supervise the whole building in provisions of cleanness and maitanance.

  • To supervise Office expenditure.

  • To make arrangement for staff travelling on duties.

  • To supervise discipline of employees.

  • To make sure that all inventory are well prepared.

(vi) Account section:

Function of Account section:

  • Follow up revenue of each cash flow from treasury.

  • To make payment to the respected supplies.

  • Posting Accounting records in books accounts.

  • To approve payment.
